About Us

i2P Engineering Solutions helps transform ideas into products through rigorous engineering and practical execution. We specialise in conceptual and detailed mechanical design, advanced simulation and analysis (FEA and CFD), rapid prototyping, and clear technical reporting. Beyond core engineering, i2P integrates automation and intelligent systems to ensure solutions are scalable, manufacturable, and future-ready.

Our role is to bridge innovation and industry by providing focused engineering consultancy that reduces risk, validates performance early, and accelerates the journey from concept to deployable product.
